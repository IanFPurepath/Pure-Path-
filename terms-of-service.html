How the App Works

PurePath uses your device camera to capture images of rooms in your home. Those images are processed by an AI model to identify materials present (e.g., cookware, bedding, bathroom fixtures) and estimate potential microplastic or chemical exposure risk. The App generates:

  • A per-room Exposure Score (0–100);
  • A whole-home weighted Exposure Score;
  • A material analysis and Exposure Report;
  • Personalised safe-swap product recommendations based on your scan results and behavioural questionnaire responses.

Scores and recommendations are probabilistic estimates derived from material science research and AI analysis of visual data. They are intended as educational guidance only. Results may vary based on image quality, lighting, room conditions, and the inherent limitations of AI-based visual analysis.
