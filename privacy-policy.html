Privacy Policy for PurePath

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

App Name: PurePath

Company: PurePath

Contact Email: community@purepath.digital

PurePath respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, website, services, and related features.

PurePath is an educational wellness and lifestyle app that helps users scan and review kitchen items, bedding items, and bathroom items for potential material-related concerns, including possible microplastic or toxin-risk indicators. PurePath does not provide medical advice, diagnostic advice, regulatory safety certification, or professional environmental testing.

By using PurePath, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

If you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password or login credentials
  • Account preferences
  • Subscription or purchase status

We use this information to create and manage your account, provide access to app features, and communicate with you about your account.

B. Photos, Camera, and Scan Data

PurePath may allow you to take or upload photos of items such as:

  • Kitchen tools, cookware, dishes, cups, cutlery, and serving utensils
  • Bedding, pillows, mattresses, blankets, comforters, and sheets
  • Bathroom items such as shower curtains, bath mats, towels, personal care containers, toiletry items, and bathroom accessories
  • Other household items submitted for scanning

We use these images to provide educational scan results, risk indicators, item analysis, and suggested safer swaps. Photos or uploaded images may be processed by our technology systems or third-party service providers to generate scan results. We do not use your photos to identify you personally unless you intentionally include personal information in the image. You should avoid uploading photos that include faces, government identification, financial documents, private messages, children, or other sensitive personal information.

C. Scan Results and App Activity

We may collect information about how you use the app, including:

  • Type of scan selected, such as Kitchen, Bedding, or Bathroom
  • Scan history
  • Risk score or result category
  • Safe swap recommendations viewed
  • Saved items
  • App feature usage
  • Button clicks and navigation activity
  • Error logs and diagnostic data

We use this information to operate the app, improve scan quality, fix bugs, understand user behaviour, and improve the app experience.

D. Device and Technical Information

We may collect technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location based on IP address
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Log data

We use this information for security, analytics, troubleshooting, fraud prevention, and app performance improvements.

E. Payment and Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, paid scan, premium report, or other digital feature through the App Store, payment is processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may receive limited purchase-related information such as:

  • Subscription status
  • Purchase date
  • Renewal status
  • Product purchased
  • Transaction identifier

We use this information to manage premium access and subscription features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the app and its features
  • Process kitchen, bedding, and bathroom scans
  • Generate educational scan results
  • Provide risk indicators and safer swap suggestions
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Save scan history
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscriptions and premium access
  • Improve app performance and accuracy
  • Monitor usage and app functionality
  • Detect bugs, abuse, fraud, or security issues
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Important Disclaimer About Scan Results

PurePath provides general educational information based on user-submitted images, visible item characteristics, product information, and available material-risk indicators. PurePath does not:

  • Diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition
  • Provide medical advice
  • Certify whether a product is safe or unsafe
  • Guarantee the presence or absence of microplastics, toxins, chemicals, or contaminants
  • Replace professional testing, product lab analysis, or advice from qualified experts

Scan results are estimates and may be incomplete or inaccurate. You should use PurePath as a general educational tool only.

4. Camera and Photo Permissions

PurePath may request access to your camera or photo library.

We use camera access so you can scan items directly inside the app. We use photo library access so you can upload selected images from your device for analysis. PurePath only accesses photos or camera content that you choose to provide or permit through your device settings. You can manage camera and photo permissions through your device settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help operate PurePath. These providers may assist with:

  • Cloud hosting
  • Image processing
  • Artificial intelligence analysis
  • App analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • Authentication
  • Payment validation
  • Customer support
  • Email delivery

These service providers may process personal information only as needed to provide services to us. Examples of third-party providers we may use include Apple for App Store payments and subscriptions, AI model providers for scan analysis, cloud hosting providers for app infrastructure and storage, authentication providers for account login, analytics providers for app usage measurement, and crash reporting providers for error monitoring. This section should be updated with the exact providers used in the final app build before launch.

6. Artificial Intelligence Processing

PurePath may use artificial intelligence or automated systems to review user-submitted images and generate educational scan results.

AI-generated results may be based on visible image characteristics, user input, product category, item type, material indicators, and other available information. AI systems can make mistakes. Results should not be treated as professional, medical, environmental, chemical, or regulatory advice. By using scan features, you understand that your submitted images and related scan data may be processed by automated systems to provide app functionality.

7. How We Store and Protect Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials private and secure.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, operate our business, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and improve our services.

Scan images and scan history may be stored so users can view past results, unless deleted by the user or removed according to our retention practices. We may retain limited information where required for legal, accounting, fraud prevention, tax, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.

9. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

If you create an account, you may request deletion of your account and associated personal information.

  • Using the account deletion feature inside the app, if available
  • Contacting us at community@purepath.digital

When you request account deletion, we will delete or de-identify personal information associated with your account unless we are legally required or permitted to keep certain information, such as purchase records, fraud prevention records, tax records, security logs, or records needed to resolve disputes. Some backup copies may remain temporarily in our systems before being deleted according to our normal backup schedule.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Object to certain uses of your information
  • File a complaint with a privacy regulator

To make a privacy request, contact us at community@purepath.digital. We may need to verify your identity before completing your request.

11. Children’s Privacy

PurePath is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information.

12. Marketing Communications

If you choose to receive marketing emails, we may send you updates, educational content, product announcements, promotions, or app-related news.

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us. We may still send non-marketing messages related to your account, purchases, security, or service updates.

13. Push Notifications

PurePath may ask for permission to send push notifications. Notifications may include:

  • Scan reminders
  • App updates
  • Saved item reminders
  • Educational tips
  • Subscription or account notices

You can turn off push notifications through your device settings at any time.

14. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed in countries outside your province, state, or country of residence.

These countries may have privacy laws that differ from the laws in your location. By using PurePath, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

15. Do Not Track and Tracking

PurePath does not sell your personal information.

PurePath does not currently use third-party advertising identifiers or track users across apps and websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes. If this changes, we will update this Privacy Policy and request any permissions required by Apple and applicable privacy laws. At this time, PurePath is intended to use data mainly to provide app functionality, scan results, analytics, support, and app improvement.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we may notify you through the app, by email, or by updating the effective date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of PurePath after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
